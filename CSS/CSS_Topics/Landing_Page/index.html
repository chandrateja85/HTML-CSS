Welcome To World Of Thar Jeeps

The Mahindra Thar is a 4-seater SUV that comes in 13 variants and three engine options. It has a ground clearance of 226 mm and a mileage of 15.2 kmpl. The Thar is available with both automatic and manual transmission.Known for their distinctive design, open-air capabilities, and off-road prowess, Jeeps have garnered a dedicated fan base worldwide. Whether navigating challenging terrains or cruising city streets, Jeeps are celebrated for their durability, go-anywhere attitude, and a timeless sense of freedom associated with the spirit of exploration
